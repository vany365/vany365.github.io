Wrong Network/Chain

In order to use Picklz, you need to point your MetaMask Wallet's Network connection to the Ethereum Mainnet:

  1. Open MetaMask and click on the Network selection dropdown.
  2. Click on "Ethereum Mainnet" to switch to the correct network.

Your wallet will now be connected to the Ethereum Mainnet. You can always use this dialog to switch back to your previous network.
